Services

We strive to be distinguished by our quality of service and hospitality.

Kitchens and Bathrooms

When we clean bathrooms, we really clean bathrooms. Staring with sink and making the porcelain sparkle and making our way to every spot, including the shower head.

Complete Vacuuming

We typically save vacuuming for last to ensure that all of the dirt and grime that trickled down to the ground while dusting will get picked up by the vacuum cleaner.

Mopping

Hardwood, laminate, tile floors? We can mop any kind of floor with care, carefully going around edges and ensuring we use the right material for different types of floors.

Making Beds

Made neatly enough to win a drill sergeant’s approval, topped with perfectly positioned shams and throw pillows, and finished with a throw blanket folded just-so at the foot of the bed – when we are done making your beds they'll look too good to mess up when it’s time to go to sleep!

Complete Dusting of all Surfaces

Dust is everyone's enemy. With our dusting service we ensure that every spot around your home is covered and dust free.

Deep, Detailed Cleaning

Sometimes you just need a deep cleaning. We got you covered too. Our deep cleaning services covers evey area of you home including blinds, windows, floors, and bathrooms.

About

Duster Magic has been in business for a while now

  • 1999

    Humble Beginnings

    Everyone starts somewhere. In my case it was a one housekeeper show, cleaning for a couple of clients.

  • 2002

    Word Got Around

    At first I had no intent of expanding to more clients but word got around and in 2002 and my clientele grew.

  • 2006

    Transition to Full Service

    We kept adding clients as word of mouth kept spreading and in 2006 I transitioned a full service housekeeping company, licensed and insured.

  • 2011

    More Growth

    In 2011 I begun work around the clock, full-time, committed to my clients. Most of the clients I have today are the same clients we start out with years ago, adding a few here and there as my schedule permits.

    Of Our
    Story!
